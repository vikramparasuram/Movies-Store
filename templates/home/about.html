My name is Vikram, a third year CS student and I am very excited for this class. I will explain more in the demo video of how this works

This store was made through Django, I used a lot of the textbook to set this up. Users that are registered can browse movies, add them to a cart, place orders, and share reviews with the community.

  • Register and log in to manage your account
  • Search movies by title
  • Add movies to your cart and place orders
  • Write, edit, and delete reviews
  • Track your order history

This project was developed step-by-step following our class textbook Django 5 for the Impatient.
